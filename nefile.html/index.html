Featured Destinations

Golden Bridge – Da Nang

One of Vietnam's most iconic attractions, located in Ba Na Hills. The bridge is famous for its giant stone hands appearing to lift the walkway above the mountains.

Ha Long Bay

A UNESCO World Heritage Site known for emerald waters, towering limestone islands, and traditional floating villages.

Quick Facts

  • 🌉 Golden Bridge is located in Ba Na Hills, Da Nang.
  • 🏔 Ba Na Hills sits high in the mountains with stunning panoramic views.
  • 🚤 Ha Long Bay contains thousands of limestone islands and islets.
  • 🌏 Both destinations are among Vietnam's most visited tourist attractions.
  • 📸 They are popular locations for photography and sightseeing.
