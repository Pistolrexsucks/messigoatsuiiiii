About Germany

Germany is located in Central Europe and is one of the world's leading countries in technology, engineering, science, and culture. Its capital city is Berlin, and it has a population of over 84 million people. Germany is famous for its beautiful castles, forests, historic cities, and annual Oktoberfest celebrations.

History of Germany

Germany has a long and fascinating history that dates back over 2,000 years. The region was originally home to Germanic tribes before becoming part of the Roman Empire's frontier.


In 1871, Germany became a unified nation under Chancellor Otto von Bismarck. During the 20th century, Germany played a major role in both World War I and World War II. After World War II, Germany was divided into East Germany and West Germany.


On October 3, 1990, Germany was reunified following the fall of the Berlin Wall, marking a new chapter in its history. Today, Germany is one of the strongest economies in Europe and a leader in innovation, education, and environmental sustainability.
